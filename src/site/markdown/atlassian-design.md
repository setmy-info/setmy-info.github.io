# Atlassian Design System

## Information

`Atlassian Design System` is Atlassian's design-system guidance and component ecosystem for building product
experiences aligned with tools such as `Jira`, `Confluence`, and related Atlassian interfaces.

In practice, it combines design principles, content guidance, foundations, components, and implementation resources to
help teams create products that feel consistent with the broader Atlassian ecosystem.

It is especially relevant when a team works within or alongside Atlassian-style product experiences, or when it wants
to study a mature example of a design system built for collaborative software products.

### Main Functionalities and Features

* **Design Foundations**: Covers color, typography, spacing, iconography, and motion.
* **Component Guidance**: Documents common interface patterns and reusable product components.
* **Content and Communication Guidance**: Includes writing and messaging considerations alongside UI design.
* **Implementation Support**: Connects design rules with engineering resources and patterns.
* **Product Consistency**: Helps multiple teams build interfaces that feel part of one ecosystem.

### How `Atlassian Design System` Fits Into Product Development

It usually sits between:

* brand and product principles,
* reusable components and patterns,
* and engineering implementation standards.

It is often useful when:

* products have collaborative, workflow-heavy, or enterprise-style interaction models,
* a team wants a documented design-system reference,
* or consistency across many product areas matters.

### Common Use Cases

* Study how a mature SaaS platform organizes a design system.
* Build interfaces inspired by structured collaborative-product patterns.
* Standardize foundations and component usage across teams.

### Practical Notes

* As with other design systems, the value comes from shared adoption and governance, not only from copying visuals.
* Teams should distinguish Atlassian-specific branding from the more general design-system lessons it provides.
