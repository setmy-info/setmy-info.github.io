# Joel Spolsky

## Information

`Joel Spolsky` is a software developer, writer, and entrepreneur best known for his long-running blog `Joel on Software`
and for co-founding `Fog Creek Software`, `Stack Overflow`, `Stack Exchange`, and later `Glitch`.

He is widely recognized in software engineering discussions because his essays helped popularize practical topics such
as software project management, developer productivity, hiring, product thinking, and the day-to-day realities of
building software teams and companies.

In practice, `Joel Spolsky` is often referenced not as a technical framework or tool, but as an influential voice in
the software industry whose writing shaped how many developers and engineering managers think about quality,
maintainability, business trade-offs, and team culture.
