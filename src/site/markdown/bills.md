# Bills

## Information

Monthly recurring bills and service providers for household and business expenses. This page tracks the service
portals and contact points for regular financial obligations.

### Email-based bills

Some providers send bills only by email:

* Electricity (if not on portal autopay)
* Other service-specific invoices

### Tips

* Set up autopay (e-arve) through your bank for recurring bills to avoid late fees.
* Most Estonian utility portals support ID card, Mobile-ID, and Smart-ID login.
* Check each portal for e-invoice (e-arve) activation — it replaces paper mail.
