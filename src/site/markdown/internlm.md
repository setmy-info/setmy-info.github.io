# InternLM

## Information

`InternLM` from `Shanghai AI Lab` includes Apache 2.0 licensed variants and is often relevant for research,
experimentation, and scientific or academic-style usage where open licensing matters.

## Common use cases

* research and experimentation,
* academic or scientific workflows,
* and open-license deployments where license clarity is important.

## Practical note for Ollama users

As with other model families, confirm whether the required `InternLM` variant is currently available in the `Ollama`
ecosystem and under which model tag.
