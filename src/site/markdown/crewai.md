# CrewAI

## Information

`CrewAI` is a framework for orchestrating collaborative, role-based AI agents.

It is designed around the idea of a team of agents with different responsibilities working together to complete a
larger task.

### What is it for?

Typical `CrewAI` use cases include:

* multi-agent workflows with assigned roles
* research, planning, and execution pipelines
* task delegation between specialized agents
* autonomous or semi-autonomous business workflows
* prototyping agent teams with clear responsibilities

## Usage

`CrewAI` is often used when the mental model of a cooperating team of agents is more useful than a single chain or a
single prompt-driven assistant.
