## Tire Size and Load/Speed Rating Explained

**215/65R16 98H – meaning**

**215**
– Tire width in millimeters (215 mm)

**65**
– Aspect ratio: sidewall height is 65% of the tire width

**R**
– Tire construction type: radial

**16**
– Rim diameter in inches (16")

**98**
– Load index
– Maximum load per tire: approx. 750 kg

**H**
– Speed index
– Maximum approved speed: up to 210 km/h
